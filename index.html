  • Shopify admin access to upload and publish themes.
  • The PawPantry-1.0.0.zip file from the Theme Store or your developer.
ℹ️
Online Store 2.0

PawPantry uses JSON templates and app blocks — no extra apps required to display core features.

Installation

1

Upload the theme zip

In your Shopify admin go to Online Store → Themes → Add theme → Upload zip file and select PawPantry-1.0.0.zip.

2

Preview before publishing

Click Preview to see the theme with your store's own products. Use the theme editor to adjust settings before going live.

3

Publish

When you're happy, click Publish. Your store switches to PawPantry immediately — no downtime.

4

Customise via the theme editor

Go to Online Store → Themes → Customise to adjust colours, fonts, sections, and content. See Theme settings for the full reference.

💡
Tip — match the demo look

Use product images with transparent or white backgrounds and the recommended brand colours in Colours to replicate the demo store.

Theme settings

All global settings available under Online Store → Themes → Customise → Theme settings. Changes apply store-wide.

ℹ️

Changes to theme settings take effect immediately in the preview and are saved when you click Save in the theme editor.

Accessibility tip

When customising color_coral, ensure at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio against white text (WCAG AA). Tools like WebAIM Contrast Checker can help.

Typography

Choose from curated font pairings. All fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, subset to Latin characters for performance.

🔤 Typography — 2 settings
font_heading
Heading font
Fredoka
Options: Fredoka (recommended) · Poppins · Nunito
font_body
Body font
Outfit
Options: Outfit (recommended) · Inter · DM Sans

Font pairing guide

  • Fredoka + Outfit — default; playful and modern, ideal for pet brands.
  • Poppins + Inter — clean and geometric; suits a more premium or clinical feel.
  • Nunito + DM Sans — rounded and friendly; great for family-oriented brands.

Announcement bar

A dismissible banner above the header. Ideal for promotions, delivery notices, and seasonal messages.

📢 Announcement bar — 3 settings
show_announcement
Show bar
Enabled
Toggle the announcement bar on or off across the entire store.
announcement_text
Announcement text

Keep under 80 characters to avoid wrapping on mobile. Emojis are supported.
announcement_link
Announcement link
—
Makes the full banner clickable. Leave blank for a static, non-linked banner.

Cart

Controls the free delivery progress bar and delivery charge shown in the cart.

🛒 Cart — 2 settings
free_delivery_threshold
Free delivery threshold
40
Enter in your store's base currency units (e.g. 40 = £40). The cart shows a progress bar toward this value.
standard_delivery_charge
Standard delivery charge
395
Enter in the smallest currency unit (pence / cents). 395 = £3.95. Shown when the free threshold hasn't been reached.
⚠️
These settings control display only

You must also set up matching shipping rates in Shopify Admin → Settings → Shipping and delivery for orders to actually qualify for free shipping.

Example values by currency

Currency free_delivery_threshold standard_delivery_charge
GBP — £40 free, £3.95 standard 40 395
EUR — €50 free, €4.99 standard 50 499
USD — $60 free, $5.99 standard 60 599
